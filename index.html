About Us

We are team of experienced and talanted teachers and lecturers.

Study anytime, anywhere, on any device.

Because your planning is not always perfect, you need to be able to study whenever, wherever. Just practice your exam one last time on your tablet or phone while you're on the go.

My Mentor is a web-based notes sharing and management system which help student and teacher to share their notes effectively, it reduces the wasting of time in manually distributing notes to each individual. It overcomes. It greatly overcomes the lack of availability and converts the old manual paperwork to a fully automated and managed online system. My Mentor allows its users to securely register and login to their individual accounts and create, read, update and deleted notes according to their needs. It provides notes to everyone in a very secure manner. Multiple user can work in this system at a same time under centralized supervision by administrator. It is very useful notes circulation system for school, colleges and other institutes to manage their notes effectively.

  • Study anytime, anywhere, on any device.
